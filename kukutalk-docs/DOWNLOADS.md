- **Node.js (LTS)** — https://nodejs.org/en/download/

- **Git** — https://git-scm.com/downloads  (pobieranie)

  Uruchomienie na Dockerze: https://redis.io/docs/latest/operate/oss_and_stack/install/install-stack/docker/

- **Visual Studio Code** — https://code.visualstudio.com/download
